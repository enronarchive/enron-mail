Subject: TE Restriction for Oct 18-19
October 18, 2001

An unplanned outage at the ATHENS (ATH) Ohio Compressor station has
occurred. Due to high throughput levels already in the system and the
restriction at ATH, TEXAS EASTERN TRANSMISSION, LP (TE) will not be
accepting physical increases for Gas Day October 18, 2001 upstream of
ATH. This would include the entire Access Area, Market Zone 1 30 Inch &
24 Inch, Market Zone 2 upstream of the ATH compressor station. Due Pipe
makeup cannot be accepted for Gas Day October 19,2001. These
restrictions may be extended through the weekend. Meter operators are
reminded to flow at scheduled volumes.

Should you have any questions, please contact your Operations Account
Manager.
