Subject: Holiday Terms for NEXT DAY Strip (N.A. Natural Gas)

The following terms will be recognized on the ICE System for trading of North American Natural Gas for the upcoming holiday season. !

!

Fri Dec21: NEXT DAY = Dec22-26 !

Mon Dec24: NEXT DAY = Dec25-26 (Canada only) !

Mon Dec24: SAME DAY = Dec24-26 (Canada only) !

Wed Dec26: NEXT DAY = Dec27 (normal) !

Thu Dec27: NEXT DAY = Dec28 (normal) !

Fri Dec28: NEXT DAY = Dec29-31 (normal; Fri afternoon trades Jan1-2) !

Mon Dec31: NEXT DAY = Jan1-2 !

!

Please make use of the Bal Month & Custom Strips for any terms that do not conform to the above standards. !
