Recap of today's meeting covering Transco's Flash Gas.

Problem:
Flash gas purchase by ENA from TDC not recorded(pathed) in Unify
Commodity gas pathed in Unify and actualized in Unify, but actualized volumes
may have included flash gas volumes for various months in order to tie out
with the pipeline
Transco paid ENA for Imbalance gas(Cashin)
ENA never paid TDC for Flash gas

Solution:
Logistics will create Deal Ticket to capture Flash Gas previously not
recorded(pathed)
Deal #745628 March 2000 - Feb. 2001(East Desk)
Deal #745637 March 2001 - until (Gulf 1 - Desk)
Logistics will create paths to capture Flash Gas activity for all prior
months(paths will be of actual flow amount per TDC Statement - refer to
Alton's worksheet)
The new paths will not be to any Internal/External Pools, but to K#4983 to
capture imbalance amount
Alton will then actualize volumes based on Transco Pipeline Statement(they
should match TDC invoiced amount)
Alton will verify that all cash out' s for prior months tie to Transco's
pipeline statement. In addition Alton will confirm that the imbalance did not
include any Flash gas volumes in the calculations
Alton will reverse out any Manual payment made to Transco(if any?) and
replace it with Unify System adjusted entries
Mary Ellenberger will verify rates to be paid to TDC with the trader and pay
TDC for Flash Gas once approved
Going forward logistic will create pathes capturing Flash Gas volumes
Target dated of completion no later than April 25, 2001
