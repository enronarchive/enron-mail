Don't Panic
</font>
<br>
<br>
<font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2" color="#000000">
Given the recent headlines regarding anthrax infection, is it a good idea to take antibiotics just in case? Officials say no.
<p>
The FDA is discouraging people from taking antibiotics such as ciprofloxacin as a prophylactic measure. Taking antibiotics without proper diagnosis of a condition could speed the development of antibiotic-resistant forms of infection, as well as put a strain on supplies.

<br>
<br>
RealAge Cost: Taking too many medications or taking them improperly can make your RealAge as much as 4.5 years older.
