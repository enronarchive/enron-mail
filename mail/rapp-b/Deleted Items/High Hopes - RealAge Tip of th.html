<b>Tip of the Day</b>
<br>
High Hopes
<br>
<br>
If you're trying to lose weight or recover from illness, having <br>high expectations may help you get the results you want.
<br><br>
A recent review of medical literature revealed that <br>
patients who have positive expectations tend to fare better <br>
than patients who are less hopeful about reaching their goals.<br>
Researchers speculate that positive expectations may trigger <br>
a physical healing response as well as motivate patients to <br>
stick with prescribed therapies.
<br>
