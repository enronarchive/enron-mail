<b>Tip of the Day</b>
<br>
Nix Nausea
<br>
<br>
Do you occasionally suffer from a queasy stomach? There<br>may be a quick and inexpensive way to get relief.
<br><br>
A recent study revealed that acupressure may help relieve <br>
nausea. In the study, pregnant women experiencing morning <br>
sickness wore acupressure bands on an area of the forearm <br>
just above the wrist, which is thought to relieve nausea when<br>
stimulated. After one day of wearing the bands, the majority<br>
of the women experienced significant relief from their symptoms.<br>
