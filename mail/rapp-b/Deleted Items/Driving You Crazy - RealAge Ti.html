<br>
Driving You Crazy
<br>
<br>
Are you hitting the road for the New Year's holiday? Do your <br>
health a favor and leave aggressive driving habits at home.
<br><br>
A recent study has revealed that people who are in the habit<br>
of driving aggressively may exhibit greater physiological<br>
sensitivity to driving stresses compared to people who don't<br>
drive aggressively. In the study, aggressive drivers tended<br>
to have tenser muscles and higher blood pressure when<br>
exposed to simulated driving tasks.
