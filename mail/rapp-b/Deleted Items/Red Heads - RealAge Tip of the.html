<br>
Red Heads
<br>
<br>
You can make a more nutritious salad by sprinkling in<br>some red cabbage.
<br><br>
One cup of chopped red cabbage will add about 50 <br>milligrams of vitamin C to your salad, and will add fiber, <br>folate, and potassium, as well. What's more, some studies have <br>revealed that a high intake of cruciferous veggies such as <br>cabbage may be protective against certain kinds of cancer, <br>including bladder and breast cancer.<br>
<br>
