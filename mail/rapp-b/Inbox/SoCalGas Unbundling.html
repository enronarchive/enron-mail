Significantly for Transwestern is the section on Intrastate Transmission Capacity and the Open Season process. This can be found on pages 41-58 with the Open Season discussion on pages 44 and 45. On pages 126 and 140, the commission directs SoCal to include an additional 375mm/d of backbone (including receipt point) capacity in the open season process. This is important because it includes the 50mm/d at Needles (per SoCal's exhibits in the system adequacy docket). In the Comprehensive Settlement, Appendix B, North Needles is listed as having capacity of 750mm/d of Primary Access Rights from Transwestern. It would be my recommendation that we need to encourage SoCal to revise this Appendix B as directed by the Commission to include the 50mm/d as capacity with Primary Access Rights from Transwestern as they had previously indicated in the Systems Adequacy Proceeding.
