Meditation Primer

Studies show that practicing mindfulness meditation may
significantly reduce stress, anxiety, and depression.

One way to practice mindfulness meditation is to merely
sit quietly for 10 to 20 minutes while concentrating on a
single object or on the flow of your breath. When your mind
begins to wander, note the new thought or sensation and
then gently bring your concentration back to your original
focus.

RealAge Benefit: Taking care of your emotional health and
