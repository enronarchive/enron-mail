<b>Tip of the Day</b>
<br>
In Good Time
<br>
<br>
If your New Year's resolutions include losing weight, give <br>yourself the best chance to succeed by making changes<br>gradually.
<br><br>
In one study of people who were successful at losing<br>
weight, making gradual changes over a long period of time <br>
appeared to be one of the keys to keeping the weight off.<br>
Instead of drastically reducing your calorie or fat intake,<br>
make small, sustainable, incremental changes to your diet.
<br>
<br>
RealAge Benefit: Maintaining your weight and body mass <br>index at a desirable level can make your RealAge as much <br>as 6 years younger.
