Enron AstroWorld tickets are $20.56 (Retail $50), which includes admission for one, plus a $7 meal ticket to one of five restaurants at AstroWorld.

Enron families will be able to purchase unlimited tickets for use anytime during the 2001 season.

Tickets will be on sale at Enron's Federal Credit Union, EB0399. Visit the credit union in person to make your purchases. AstroWorld tickets will not be mailed. Enron employees who are not already Enron FCU members may pay by cash or money order only. All Enron Employees are encouraged to take advantage of this great summer fun deal and may purchase tickets through August 30th.
