3) Limited spill to help fish passage will continue
The Bonneville Power Administration and other federal agencies decided on June 1 to continue the current level of limited spill operations for fish passage at Bonneville, The Dalles, John Day and McNary dams. The agencies will monitor the situation and have not set a specific date for how long spill will continue.
