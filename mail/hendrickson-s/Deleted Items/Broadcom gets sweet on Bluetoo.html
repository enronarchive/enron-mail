Read Full Story [IMAGE] [IMAGE] Broadcom gets sweet on Bluetooth Chipmaker Broadcom announced Monday a new chip for wireless devices that conforms to the Bluetooth standard, which could reduce costs for device makers and help jumpstart the Bluetooth wireless networking market. The BCM2033 is a single chip that runs on Bluetooth technology, which allows wireless devices such as handheld computers, mobile phones and even laptops to communicate with each other within a distance of about 30 feet.
