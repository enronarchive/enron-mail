GSB FINANCIAL SERVICES INC. ANNOUNCES COMPREHENSIVE
MARKETING PLAN FOR OPTIMUMPAY CARD

LOS ANGELES, Jan 29, 2002 -- GSB Financial Services Inc.
(GSBF) today announced that it had initiated a
comprehensive marketing and distribution plan for its
product "OptimumPay" a pre-paid cash storage card.

OptimumPay functions in any electronic payment environment.
It can act as a credit or debit card and it also can work
with the ATM banking network. In addition, the OptimumPay
card can be customized to fit customers' special needs.

Rodney Ray, senior vice president of sales and marketing
for GSBF, said, "Today we are launching an innovative
marketing program for the OptimumPay card. We will be
targeting the direct response, network marketing and pre-
paid phone card industries. These industries represent very
large communities of purchasers with well established
distribution channels.

"We believe that our OptimumPay card will provide these
industries with a unique financial solution which will
enable merchants and customers alike higher profits and
better service."

"With the initiation of this marketing program for the
OptimumPay card, GSBF should be able to tap into the multi-
million dollar businesses represented by the direct
response, pre-paid phone card and network marketing
industries," said Steve Browning, CEO of GSBF. "And, we are
very confident that Mr. Ray with his direct response
marketing background will quickly generate sales for the
company."

About GSB Financial Services Inc.

GSBF is a 21st Century financial group. It employs cutting
edge technology to take advantage of existing and emerging
e-commerce and e-finance opportunities. GSBF expressly
looks to the banking and finance industries to increase its
market share.

GSBF markets to existing credit card processors, credit
card issuers and high-technology companies looking for new
and innovative means to increase profits and customer
satisfaction. GSBF has offices in the United States and
Canada.

About Optimum Pay USA Inc.

Optimum Pay is a global electronic payment provider that
delivers market leading, value added, security-based
payment systems. Customer solutions incorporating Optimum
Pay enable both enterprise and individual users to initiate
and manage all of their payments via traditional "card"
products, the Internet, or emerging wireless connections.
Optimum Pay's payment approach and architecture provides a
low-cost, high-value payment mechanism for businesses and
merchants. Users also benefit greatly from the use of
Optimum Pay by reaping the rewards of integrated
incentives, rebates, and discounts as well as being able to
consolidate and simplify management of their financial
payment accounts.

Unlike most other payment systems that only provide simple,
lower value currency movement, Optimum Pay employs a
process of "Value Integration," which results in a higher
value proposition for all customers and users. Moreover,
Optimum Pay's security-based system enables companies to
reduce their costs of doing business associated with fraud.
