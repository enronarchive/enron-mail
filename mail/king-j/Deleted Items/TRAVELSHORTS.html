TRAVELSHORTS.COM INC. TAKES ANOTHER STEP TOWARDS ITS QUEST
TO ACQUIRE OIL & GAS ASSETS

VANCOUVER, British Columbia--Nov. 20, 2001--
Travelshorts.com Inc. (the ``Company'') (OTCBB: TLSH)
announced today that it has enlisted the services of Edison
Oil and Gas Ltd. to evaluate a number of oil and gas
exploration and development properties in the USA.

The management of Edison Oil and Gas Ltd. has over 30 years
of oil and gas exploration and development experience in
the states of Texas, California, Oklahoma, Wyoming, Kansas,
Colorado and Montana.

TRAVELSHORTS.COM INC. ANNOUNCES INVESTIGATION OF OIL AND
GAS RESOURCES BUSINESS OPPORTUNITIES

VANCOUVER, British Columbia--Nov. 19, 2001--
Travelshorts.com Inc. (the ``Company'') (OTCBB: TLSH)
announced today that it has started to investigate various
oil and gas resources business opportunities primarily in
the Western United States.

As part of its investigation the Company is presently
reviewing and considering the independent analysis and
evaluation of various oil and gas properties. The Company
will continue this investigative effort as appropriate
properties are submitted for participation.
