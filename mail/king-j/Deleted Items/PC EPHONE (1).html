PC-EPHONE HONORED AT COMDEX FALL 2001

SAN DIEGO--Nov. 15, 2001--PC-EPhone, Inc. (OTCBB: PCPH
Frankfurt: NL1, WKN 589864) is pleased to announce that it
has been honored at Comdex Fall 2001 in Las Vegas by
industry experts. On the first day of Comdex, the PC-EPhone
device won the Positive Image News & Reviews Editor's
Choice Award. The article referred to the PC-EPhone device
as ``light years ahead of the rest'' and stated that ``PC-
EPhone has bestowed upon us a historical moment to view a
product of such magnitude in design and innovation.'' The
full article can be accessed at
http://www.positiveimagenews.com/comdex/pcephone/index.html.


In addition, for an unprecedented second year the PC-EPhone
device was selected a finalist for ``Best of Comdex''. This
year the PC-EPhone device was nominated in the
Mobile/Handheld System category along with products from
Toshiba and Handspring. Last year the device was the
finalist in the ``Best of Comdex'' Wireless/Mobile Product
category.

``We are pleased and honored to have been selected and
nominated by leading industry experts for such
recognition,'' said David Meltzer, CEO and President, of PC-
EPhone, Inc. ``We are proud of our development partner,
Cyberbank Corp., and their dedication to making such fine,
world-class wireless devices.''

About PC-EPhone, Inc.

PC-EPhone, Inc., through its wholly owned subsidiary, is
the exclusive U.S. and Canadian distributor of the PC-
EPhone device and the Company also has the right to co-
develop Cyberbank Corp.'s (the developer of the PC-EPhone
and a major shareholder of the Company) future devices. The
PC-EPhone is the world's first wireless convergent device
which combines the capabilities of a handheld personal
computer with full Internet access, a cellular phone, a
PDA, and an organizer all-in-one.

http://www.pc-ephone.com
